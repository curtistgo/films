Curtis

The Filmmaker

Curtis is a New York City based filmmaker who studied the art of dramatic writing and acting at Stony Brook University. He continued his study of filmmaking as a grad student at New School University. Coming from a social science background, he enjoys writing stories that are steeped in social issues. However, subject matter that is light and just plain fun are great to bring to life as well. The end result of filmmaking is worth all the trouble.
